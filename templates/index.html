About JusticeAI

JusticeAI is designed to help Indian users navigate legal documents and understand basic legal concepts through technology.

Our tool uses natural language processing (NLP) to analyze documents, extract key information, and provide answers to common legal questions related to Indian law context.

  • Text summarization
